DiscoPop

  • Mobile Computing
  • Flutter/Dart
  • UI

DiscoPop is a unique mobile game designed for collocated interaction.

Each player has ammunitation that they spawn to attack their opponent, while simultaneously protecting their own lane.

The game is based on edge-to-edge technology, meaning that the users play with their devices topmost edges connected. When they spawn ammo, it travels across the edge of their device to the other players device.

For this project I was a part of the UI and gameplay design team.
