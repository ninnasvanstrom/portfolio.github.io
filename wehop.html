weHop

  • Arduino
  • Graphic Design
  • Game

weHop is a physical game platform based on a grid of interactive hexagon tiles. Each tile is connected to sensors that react to pressure, and connected to speakers and led strips that give auditory and visual feedback upon interaction. The games require physical movement, but are also constructed to involve levels of cerebral engagement.

The purpose of weHop is to increase physical activity within young children, as our stakeholder for this project is Generation Pep.

As this was a group project, the workload was divided between each member. Although all of us were somewhat involved in all stages, we were assigned with different responsibilites. My main commitment for this project was the graphical and visual design of the different UI's, such as the game display, website and poster.
